The Railson White Partnership is an independent marketing and brand development consultancy set up by Ellie Railson and Alistair White in 2006. Terry Melton joined as Creative Director in early 2007.

We draw upon broad personal experience of consumer, business to business, not for profit and public sector markets and have worked with a wide range of leading UK and international companies. We currently provide a full marketing, design and publicity service including:

- Market research & strategic planning

- Design & print

- Advertising

- Website design

- E-marketing

- Media relations

- Event management

We work for businesses of all sizes and have significant current in-depth experience of the social care sector.
